Our Story

Designed for Everyday Icons.

Since 2019

Built by people who wear their work.

Urban Threads started as a small studio project between three friends who were tired of choosing between quality and design. We wanted essentials that looked considered, felt premium, and lasted longer than a season.

Today, every piece we make goes through multiple fit sessions, fabric trials and durability tests before it ever reaches the collection. We work directly with mills we trust, and we keep our supply chain small enough to stay accountable to it.

We're not chasing trends. We're building a wardrobe of considered basics that quietly outperform everything else in your closet.

Our Mission

To design premium, considered essentials that make getting dressed effortless — without compromising on craftsmanship, comfort, or sustainability.

Our Vision

A world where premium clothing is defined by longevity and intention, not seasonal hype — where every icon feels dressed for their everyday.
